Design a modern SaaS web application called “AI Roundtable” — a multi-model reasoning platform where users submit complex topics and receive structured responses from 4 AI systems (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Grok).

The design must feel premium, intelligent, analytical, and futuristic — similar to Stripe Dashboard + Linear + Notion AI.

Use a dark theme with subtle gradients, glassmorphism cards, soft shadows, and modern typography (Inter or SF Pro style).

The UI must look like a professional AI research tool, not a chatbot.
